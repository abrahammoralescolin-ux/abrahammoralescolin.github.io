About Me

Hello! I'm Abraham Morales, a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Manufacturing Engineering with a concentration in SMART Manufacturing from Texas State University. I am passionate about finding innovative solutions to complex manufacturing challenges, from initial design for manufacturability to full-scale production.

My coursework and hands-on projects have provided me with a strong foundation in CAD/CAM software, GD&T, lean manufacturing principles, fabrication, DFM/DFA . I am eager to apply my academic knowledge and problem-solving skills to a challenging entry-level role in the manufacturing industry.
